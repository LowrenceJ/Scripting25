Character Overview

Yoichi Isagi is the main character from a sports anime/manga called Blue Lock created by Muneyuki Kaneshiro in 2018. Isagi is 17 years old and is an exceptional soccer player with a dream of becoming the number 1 striker in Japan.

Journey & Development

Isagi's journey started after his loss during his high school national qualifiers. Isagi was frustrated about it but the next day he got a letter saying that he's been invited to a soccer training camp called Blue Lock. When Isagi got there he was told that there were 299 other people and that the goal of Blue Lock is to create the number 1 striker in the world.
